Who we are

A dessert made to be remembered

PGS CanJoy started with one simple idea: what if a dessert could feel like a gift? We began crafting cheesecakes, milk cakes and indulgent cake pots into beautiful, shareable jars — each one handmade to order with the freshest ingredients and zero preservatives.

Based in Hyderabad, we take immense pride in our craft. No mass production, no shortcuts. Every jar is made with real love and real care — whether it's going straight to you or to someone very special.

From birthdays and anniversaries to festivals and gifting — CanJoy is here to make every moment a little sweeter.
